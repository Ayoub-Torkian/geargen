How to use the templates

Print this page at 100% scale (not "fit to page"). Verify by measuring a pitch circle against the printed gear with a ruler. Glue each gear onto your plywood with a thin layer of adhesive, drill the bore holes first, then cut along the outline with a bandsaw. Sand to the line.

For 6 mm plywood, tooth spacing under 7 mm will produce fragile teeth that can chip. The tool warns you if your settings are too aggressive.

Two gears mesh only if they share the same tooth spacing and pressure angle. The center distance between their shafts is shown below.
