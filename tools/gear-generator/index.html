Gear Template Generator

Print involute spur-gear templates at 1:1 scale. Set the tooth count, module (size per tooth), and pressure angle, then print and glue to your stock.

Notes

The template prints at true size — 1 mm in the SVG equals 1 mm on paper, provided your printer is set to 100% (not "fit to page"). Verify with a ruler against the cross-hairs after the first print.

Two meshing gears must share the same module and pressure angle. The center distance between them is (N₁ + N₂) × module / 2.
